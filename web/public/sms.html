Card Finder — SMS Alert Sign-Up

Card Finder is a sports-card price-tracking service. Last updated June 2026.

What you're signing up for

On the Card Finder Alerts page, you choose specific trading cards to watch. When a card you watch is listed for sale (e.g. on eBay) or comes up for auction, Card Finder sends you an automated text alert. This is the SMS opt-in for that program.

How you opt in

On our Alerts page you enter your mobile number, choose SMS as a delivery method, and agree to the consent below before any texts are sent. Here is exactly what that opt-in looks like:

Mobile Phone Number (for SMS)

Consent is not a condition of any purchase, and SMS is optional — alerts can also be delivered by email.
