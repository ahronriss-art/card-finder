Card Finder — Privacy Policy & SMS Terms

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Card Finder is a sports card price-tracking service. Users sign up on our website to receive alerts when specific trading cards are listed on marketplaces such as eBay. This page describes how we handle your information and our SMS messaging program.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information you provide to set up alerts: your email address and/or mobile phone number, the cards you choose to watch, and your alert preferences. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing.

SMS Messaging Program

By entering your mobile phone number and selecting SMS alerts on our website, you consent to receive recurring automated text messages from Card Finder about cards you have chosen to watch. Consent is not a condition of any purchase.

  • Message frequency varies based on the alerts you set up and marketplace activity.
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages.
  • Reply HELP for help, or email 26buys@gmail.com.
  • Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

How We Use Your Information

We use your contact information solely to send you the card alerts you requested. You can remove alerts or delete your account at any time from the Alerts page on our website.

Data Sharing

We use service providers (such as Twilio for SMS and SendGrid for email) only to deliver the notifications you request. We do not share your phone number or email with advertisers.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email 26buys@gmail.com.
